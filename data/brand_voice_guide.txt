GreenLeaf Gardens Brand Guide
Voice: helpful, seasonal, practical, friendly, never pushy.
Audience: home gardeners, especially beginners and hobbyists in USDA grow zones 4-8.
Messaging pillars: confidence for beginners, right plant at the right time, practical education, quality guarantee.
Avoid: exaggerated claims, fear-based urgency, unsupported medical/environmental claims.
Preferred CTA language: Start planting, Build your garden plan, See beginner picks, Shop zone-ready plants.
